About the Complex

Presentation

At the Epicenter of Activity

WAYWIN PLAZA Business Center is located in the fashionable downtown of Limassol – the main financial center of the Republic of Cyprus. The Business Center is within a three-minute drive from the see and the motorway connecting the main cities of the island and the airports of Paphos and Larnaca. Within walking distance of the business center there are offices of major financial companies and banks, showrooms of world-famous brands: Harley-Davidson, Maserati, Mercedes-Benz, and Porsche, as well as the customer service office of the electric company, the main provider of telephone services in Cyprus.

Offices, Apartments and Retail area

The Business Center's building with a total area of 5000 square meters consists of a 2-level underground parking for 150 cars, the ground floor with a comfortable lounge and a secretary, a shop of exclusive furniture and home decorations as well as a lounge area for a café or a restaurant. The upper floors (1-4) are occupied by offices and apartments.

Engineering and Technologies

The building is equipped with light-absorbing glazing, high ceilings, modern central air conditioning and a ventilation system, high-speed panoramic elevators and open verandas overlooking the sea, the city and the mountains. Solar panels on the facade of the building generate electricity, which allows you to significantly save on electricity consumption. The construction was based on modern and environmentally friendly technologies, high quality materials and well thought-out and carefully engineered life-support systems. Inside the offices there is a convenient system of suspended floors and stretch ceilings, which allows you to quickly and easily adapt the office to the needs of a particular client.

Room for your Business

We left the planning free so that owners can easily adapt the space to the features of their businesses. Shopping units are located on the first floor along the course of pedestrian traffic.
